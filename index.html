Madi accepted a last-minute contract to work as an Assistant Coordinator in Ephraim, UT, where Mac had been working for weeks. They kept in contact afterwards, and - despite 125 miles of road between them - committed to spend an evening together. As they say... the rest is history.

Ephraim

The Belltower

June 26th-27th, 2022

Snow College, Ephraim, UT

Mac invited newcomer Madi to join a campus tour group he was leading the night before their FSY session began. At one point he brought the group to the campus belltower, where he explained that Snow students become "true badgers" by kissing someone at the belltower on the night of a full moon. The following day, their coordinator asked Madi if she knew where the belltower was for one of her assignments - to which Madi replied: "Yes! Mac showed me last night." Chaos ensued, and Madi's face became bright red.

Night Watch & The Ride Home

July 1st-2nd, 2022

Following a week of collaborating with each other, Mac asked Madi to join him for night watch in order to get to know her better. They walked and talked for hours, and Mac was super impressed, so much so that he coordinated giving Madi a ride back to Provo. Unfortunately, someone else took the front seat, and Madi was crammed in the back with the luggage, deflating Mac's conversational skills.

Notable Dates

First Date - Mission Reunion

September 30th, 2022

Sandy, UT

With tight schedules but a desire to go out, Mac and Madi went to Mac's Ohio Cincinnati Mission reunion, where Madi endured endless torment and only experienced a few red faces throughout the night. First Kiss

the wee hours of November 5th, 2022

Provo, UT

After watching the cinematic masterpiece The Dark Knight on the night of their 5th date, Mac couldn't help but ask Madi about her thoughts on becoming exclusive. While Mac feared Madi would think he was trying to commit too quickly, to his surprise Madi said she was willing and ready. November 5th became an important date as they became official and shared their first kiss.
